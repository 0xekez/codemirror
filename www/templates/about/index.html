About

chmod4 lets you start a mirroring session from inside your editor and share what you're working on to a private link.

We made chmod4 because we were tired of using Zoom to share code with our group mates in classes. We wanted a convenient way to share the code we were looking at without needing to use an online editor or stream a HD video of our screen over a video call.

chmod4 is made by Noah and Zeke. If you have feedback or would like to get in touch feel free to join our Discord!
